About DemCode

Passion, innovation, and dedication coming together to create meaningful solutions.

DemCode, founded on 16 September 2025 by Demitri Steyn in Bloemfontein, South Africa, is a software development, website development, and IT support company driven by passion and innovation. As a 2nd-year Software Engineering student at Eduvos, Demitri built DemCode from a love of problem-solving and creating with technology, transforming that passion into a company dedicated to helping others succeed through digital solutions. From crafting custom software and modern, responsive websites to providing reliable IT support, DemCode is committed to delivering value-driven, creative, and practical solutions. Every project is approached with care and precision, making DemCode not just a business, but a journey of turning ideas into meaningful tools that empower people and businesses alike.
